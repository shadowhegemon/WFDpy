About Winter Field Day Logger

What is Winter Field Day?

Winter Field Day is an annual amateur radio event organized by the Winter Field Day Association (WFDA). It takes place on the last full weekend in January and emphasizes emergency preparedness and operating skills during winter conditions.

Key Features:
  • 24-hour contest (1900 UTC Saturday to 1900 UTC Sunday)
  • Emphasis on emergency power and outdoor operation
  • Categories for indoor/outdoor and power levels
  • Exchange includes category and state/province/country

About This Logger

This Flask-based logging application helps amateur radio operators track their contacts during Winter Field Day. Features include:

  • Easy contact logging with Winter Field Day specific fields
  • Real-time statistics and summaries
  • Contact history and search
  • Bootstrap-based responsive design
  • SQLite database for reliable storage

Built with Python Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Bootstrap for a clean, functional interface perfect for contest logging.
